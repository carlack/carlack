Carlack Carpet & Interior Remover 500ml

Universal Cleaner suitable for most surfaces

Carlack Carpet & Interior Remover is formulated for effective yet gentle cleaner. As a universal cleaner, it removes soiling, grease, oil, salt and etc on most surface without leaving stains. For your car interior, it can be applied to leather, carpet, upholstery, plastic, fabric and etc. Why just limit this product to your car when it can effectively removes stain on sofa, table cloth, curtain, fabric, textile in your house.

Application.

1) Spray on freely on the spot to be cleaned.

2) In case of heavy dirt, rub the solution with moist sponge or soft scrub brush.

3) Dry with soft cloth.
