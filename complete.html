Carlack Excellence Complete 500ml

A highly effective paintwork cleaner and a sealant based on synthetic polymers and 100% finest natural carnauba wax

Carlack Excellence Complete is the newest creation of the manufacturing department and combines 50 years of experience in sealant technology with the best that nature has to offer your vehicle. The latest product also represents the state of the art in one-step polymer-based paint protection technology combine with 100% finest natural carnauba wax. The unique formula Carlack Excellence Complete comprise of excellent cleaning properties, lay down an acrylic coat protection and to perfect the gloss with carnauba wax. This combination makes it so easy to achieve show car finish with minimum effort.

By using Carlack Excellence Complete, an unparalleled surface coating density is achieved that greatly improves resistance to environmental threats, such as ultraviolet radiation, acid rain and the organic acids in bird droppings and bug splatter, and mechanical damage, such as wash-induced marring and scratches.

CarLack Excellence Complete bonds well with surface like wax but doesnâ€™t penetrate or change the paint in any way unlike other brands. With heat resistance up to 160degC and UV protection, the applied CarLack Excellence Complete layer lasts much longer than other polyethylene based car care product.

Application.

1) Thoroughly wash surface as usual.

2) Apply a thin layer of Carlack Excellence Complete using a clean soft cloth. For quick detailing, the surface do not need to be dry before applying the product. For a streak- and smear-free shine the product needs to be completely dry.

3) After application, wait for the surface to become completely dry (avoid strong sunlight).

4) Wipe away dried product residues thoroughly with soft, clean microfibre cloths.

5) Suggestion for maintenance, protection can be prolonged by mixing 3 cups into 3 litres of water

Features

Comprise of a unique formulated blend of polyethylene-acrylic nano-scale polymers, UV Protection, Cleaning Agent and 100% natural carnauba wax manufactured under intense and costly methods.

Advantage

Carlack Excellence Complete cleans out deep-seated dirt and grime from painted surface and at the same time lay down the hard protection which derives from the polyethylen-acrylic properties. In addition, acrylic provides high gloss clarity and further enhance by 100% natural carnauba wax to produce mirror-like images.

Benefit

Heat resistance up to 160degC and UV protection most applicable under hot sun to prolong paintwork. Carlack Nano lasts much longer than other polyethylene based car care product and far more superior than wax, silicon based, polymer based coating. Takes less effort to wipe on/off with its water based with non-abrasive formula. Carlack Nano works well with vinyl and rubber without staining. Intense gloss is most obvious on warm solid-coloured paint finishes, such as reds and yellows, and can be enhanced further by applying a good quality pure carnauba wax on top
