Carlack Long Life 500ml

High performance acrylic-based paint sealant

Carlack Long Life represents what Carlack stands firm in its 50 years of sealing technology. It complements the layer of Carlack Nano with its concentrated formula to protect new or reconditioned paintwork by a micro-fine, but also extremely resilient polymer-film. With UV protection, it is extremely durably preserved and protected from harmful environmental effects

Carlack Long Life produces an incredibly smooth finish, which is characterised by mirror-like reflectivity, razor sharp clarity and, on metallic and pearlescent finishes that can last through many washes.

In terms of suitability, we suggest using Carlack Long Life on light to mid-tone metallic and pearlescent finishes, although it is more than capable to produce an excellent finish on most colours. Completely safe to use as it contains no chemical cleaning agents.

Application.

1) Thoroughly wash surface with Carlack Shampoo and coat it with Carlack Nano.

2) Apply a thin layer of Carlack Long Life using a clean soft cloth.

3) After application, wait for the surface to become completely dry at least 15 mins(avoid strong sunlight).

4) Wipe away dried product residues thoroughly with soft, clean microfibre cloths.

5) Suggest to recoat at the interval of 3 to 6 months for optimal protection
