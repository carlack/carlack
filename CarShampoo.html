Carlack Car Shampoo 500ml

Designed to remove the most stubborn stain, effortlessly

Carlack Shampoo Concentrate has been specially developed for frequent car washing. It contains up to 22% wash-active substances and works powerfully against dirt.The re-greasing formula protects the layers of wax and sealing already applied to the paintwork and prevents rubber seals and plastic parts from becoming brittle. Carlack Shampoo Concentrate is eco-friendly, biodegradable and extremely economical. 1-2 capfuls in 5 litres of water are sufficient for lots of foam and gentle cleaning.
