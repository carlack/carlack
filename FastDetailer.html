Carlack Fast Detailer 500ml

Water-based quick detailing spray fortified with acrylic polymers and UV protection

Carlack Fast Detailer is a fast cleaner formulated with acrylic properties to be used in between washes or prolong the life of sealed surfaces to give a freshly detailed look with its UV protection. The lubricity enable removal of slight impurities like chalk flecks, road dust and finger marks between car washes without deteriorating the paintwork. The incremental addition of acrylic polymer protection over time makes washing and drying easier, and prolongs the life of existing sealant protection almost indefinitely.Definitely the quick turnaround solution to reinstate the glossy paintwork seen in CarLack Nano.

Our busy schedules may not permit us to spend hours to give our paintwork that layers of protection. With the gentle cleaning properties, you can spend 5 to 10 mins every few days on quick detailing to have a car that looks like it's been professionally washed and waxed. Tested to lighten the yellowish discolouration on white car, the cleaning properties is unique in its class. CarLack Fast Detailer features it lubricity properties that can be used during claying to clean and lay over the initial acrylic coat for maximum protection.

Application.

1) Thoroughly wash surface as usual. For best result, suggest to prepare surface by clay bar service

2) Spray Carlack Fast Detailer on the desired surface, then wipe dry with microfiber cloth.

3) After application, wait for the surface to become completely dry (avoid strong sunlight).

4) Wipe away dried product residues thoroughly with a new soft, clean microfibre cloth
