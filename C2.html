Carlack C2 Surface Cut 500ml

Mid-performance PTFE (Teflon) based abrasive paste for light to moderate paint defects and imperfections

Carlack C2 Surface Cut is a mid performance PTFE (Teflon) based abrasive compound that removes light to moderate paint defects and imperfections with a smooth and glossy finishing. This silicone-free compound minimise dusting during the processs of polishing, and wipes off easier than any compound leaving teflon protection on your paintwork. Carlack C2 is strong enough to remove 1,600 grit sanding marks on paintwork, yet without leaving stains on the surface.

With the advancement in paint technology, we have seen ceramic coating and other water-based paint that last longer than conventional technology. While that is truely advanced in scratch resistant in its hardness, it has also pose concerns when removing the swirls, scratches, marring, and holograms that do end up in the paintwork. An excellent choice for detailing professionals and experienced enthusiasts, Carlack C2 cuts fast and deep up to 1,600 grit to remove the mild scratch, swirl, and defect on any single stage, conventional clear coat, or advanced ceramic clear coat finish on new and used vehicles.
