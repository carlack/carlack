Carlack C3 Perfect Cut and Wax 500ml

Perfect finishing polish that refines the deepest shine, clarity, and depth with Carnauba wax after heavier compounding and polishing steps

Carlack C3 Perfect Cut and Wax comprise of high tech abrasive compound that perfect your detailing work to refine the finish, remove light scuffs and scratches. Blended with Carnaube wax, it refines compound and polishing marks from prior steps, and enhances reflection, shine, and gloss on any paint finish . This silicone-free compound minimise dusting during the processs of polishing, and wipes off easier than any compound leaving the ultimate finish on any perfect detailing job. Carlack C3 is capable to remove up to 3,000 grit sanding marks on paintwork, yet without leaving stains on the surface.

Carlack C3 Perfect Cut and Wax works best with C1 Power Cut (for strongly weathered paint) and C2 Surface Cut (for light to moderate paint defects and imperfections). Using either polishing compound with C3 Perfect Cut and Wax saves the hassle of remving the dust from crack and crevices, whereby you can proceed to the next step of systematic care to coat your painwork. Get ready for that finest final polishing results worthy of any show car, garage queen, or daily driver that deserves to look its very best.
